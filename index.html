Bio

Hey there!

I am a PhD Student in CS at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. I am being advised by Anupam Joshi.

I also work as a Graduate Research Assistant for the Ebiquity lab and the Knacc lab at UMBC. I am broadly interested in Human Language Technologies and AI. My research involves natural language processing for cloud vendors and defuzzifying user opinion.

Prior to this, I was a Software Development Engineer at Amazon Development Centre in Hyderabad, India. I worked for the World Wide Defect Elimination team where my primary task was to identify customer abuse and develop automated bots for return services.

Follow me on github to find out more about my work.
